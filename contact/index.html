Contact

Corrections welcome. Seriously.

A register built from public data will contain mistakes — the honest move is making them easy to report and fast to fix. Here’s how to reach us.

Report wrong venue data

Wrong phone number, dead website, outdated photos, a venue that closed, a listing that shouldn’t be here at all — send it over and it goes to the front of the review queue:

Email: hello@theretreatregister.com

Include the venue name and what’s wrong. A link to the correct information helps but isn’t required — verification is our job, not yours.

Venue owners

If you own or manage a property in the register: listing claims open at launch, free. You’ll be able to add details, photos, and current contact information — though never buy a ranking. Want first access when claiming opens? Join the launch list and say hello at the address above.

Planners, writers, and everyone else

Questions about the register, the methodology, or the coverage data on the markets page — same address: hello@theretreatregister.com.

The Retreat Register is independently operated. Email goes to the people who actually build the register — there is no support department to get lost in.
