What a market page will give you at launch

When the full register opens, each market page is built to answer the questions planners actually compare on:

  • Getting there — nearest airports and realistic drive times.
  • Privacy — which properties offer full buyouts.
  • Staying on-site — lodging capacity, not just meeting rooms.
  • Beyond the meetings — wellness, outdoors, and team activities.
  • Pricing transparency — who publishes rates and who quotes per group, stated plainly either way.
