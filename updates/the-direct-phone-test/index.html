The fastest venue quality test: can you actually reach it?

July 15, 2026 · The Retreat Register

You’ve found a promising retreat venue online, only to spend a good part of the afternoon battling chatbots or filling out forms that vanish into the ether. It’s a common frustration, and frankly, a waste of a planner's precious time.

This digital hide-and-seek makes you wonder: does anyone actually work there? Getting a direct answer, or even a direct line, seems to be a lost art in some corners of the industry.

2,210venues verified and live
91%list a direct phone number
185publish a starting price — the rest are quote-only

We at The Retreat Register believe in plain speaking, and that includes plain contact. Our philosophy is simple: if you can’t reach them before you book, you’ll likely struggle to reach them after. It’s a bold claim, perhaps, but one we stand by.

That's why we make direct phone numbers a cornerstone of our listings. As of July 15, 2026, our directory holds 2,210 verified retreat venues, with 10 new ones cleared just since the last update. A solid 91% of these venues—that's 2,002 of 2,210—list a direct phone number. Contrast this with pricing: only 185 venues publish a starting price, as quote-only is the industry norm. We gather every bit of contact we can, from California (154 venues) and New York (100) to Louisiana (91) and Texas (86).

So, here’s your fix: use reachability as your first-pass filter. If a venue makes itself easy to talk to, that’s a good sign for future dealings. The Retreat Register aims to cut through the digital noise, connecting you directly to the people who can help you plan your next great event.
