Faith-based retreat centers: in the register, held to the same bar

July 18, 2026 · The Retreat Register

The register holds 2,125 verified retreat venues as of July 18, 2026.

Planners often find faith‑based retreat centers either missing from lists or dumped in without a real check, leaving them unsure if the site meets basic standards.

2,125venues verified and live
91%list a direct phone number
182publish a starting price — the rest are quote-only

The Retreat Register changes that by applying the same four‑question bar to every venue: real lodging, attractive grounds, professional operation, and a welcome to outside groups.

Because of this, 91% of venues (1,928 of 2,125) list a direct phone number, only 182 venues publish a starting price; pricing in this industry is quote‑only by default, and the deepest coverage today is California (162), New York (98), Louisiana (80).

If you run a faith‑based retreat, submit your property for the four‑question verification; if you’re planning, use the register’s filter to find vetted sites and skip the guesswork.
